    Membership Levels

  • NP Membership

    As a NP member, you are a non profit organization. Support will be provided for any events you plan to organize. There will be no fee collected for being a NP member.

  • Bronze Membership

    As a Bronze member, you'll get 10% discount for city monthly trainings and 5% on advertising materials. This will also give you 1 free entry to an event each month and support provided for any events you will organize.

    An annual fee of $220 will be collected for being a bronze member.

  • Silver Membership

    As a Silver member, you'll get 20% discount for city monthly trainings and 15% on advertising materials. This will also give you 2 free entry to an event each month and support provided for any events you will organize. Benefits also extends to your employees to get 15% off staying at any city choice hotels.

    An annual fee of $380 will be collected for being a silver member.

  • Gold Membership

    As a Gold member, you'll get 40% discount for city monthly trainings and 35% on advertising materials. This will also give you unlimited free entry to an event each month and support provided for any events you will organize. Benefits also extends to your employees to get 25% off staying at any city choice hotels. You will also enjoy priority pass membership for any conferences held by the city.

    An annual fee of $750 will be collected for being a gold member.
