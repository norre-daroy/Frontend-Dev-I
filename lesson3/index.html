Site Purpose

Oshawa chamber of commerce main purpose is to promote local business es and provide valuable resources for the community. This website will feature services and products to other business, provide historical information about the city, and host events. Moreover, it contains useful information about the advantages of being a member of this commerce. It includes a detailed page of requirements to join and a membership application form. Furthermore, this page will also contain extended contact information for the chamber and its board of directors.
