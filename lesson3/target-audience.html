Target Audience

Target audience are people who lives or visits the community such as the residents, business owners and tourists of Oshawa. This site target families and individuals who are looking for activities or events in the community. This site will also target those who seeks to start or promote their business. It also targets those who seeks to become a member of the chamber. Moreover, it targets those who wants to know more about the history of the city.

Personas

  1. Liza is a 38-year-old nurse who likes to groom her dog at home. She wants to know if there are any grooming services that would be best for her pet dog. She accesses the internet using her tablet over wifi. She has a bad experience with grooming services before so she prefers to know any recommendations or read reviews before visiting any.
  2. Noah is a father of an 8-year-old boy and a 12-year-old girl. He wants his kids to learn how to swim and wants to know places that offer swimming classes and have a decent size pool. He is a banker and uses his laptop to access the internet. He is usually busy so he wants something that's closer to home. He likes to get all his information quickly that includes a map for directions.

Scenarios

  1. Where can I buy products for my pet dog?
  2. What are upcoming events for this month that my family can go to?
  3. Who are the officers and board of directors of this community?
  4. Who can I contact so that I can promote my business?
  5. Where can I sign up to become a member of the chamber?
