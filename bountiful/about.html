About us

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote fresh and healthy food options to local consumers and tourists visiting the coastal areas. We feature services and products including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags. Moreover, we will also provide support for delivery and managing orders. We will also provide information about any coastal areas or areas in the southern California.

Our Story

Bountiful foods is not well known overseas, but in California coastal areas, it's a popular local business. Bountiful foods launched in 2009 which started in a local markets. Now we are expanding our business to serve more people.
